Welcome to the Rock, Paper, and Scissors game

How to Play:

Start Game:

Enter 'y' to start the game. The game will start by displaying a prompt asking you to choose rock, paper, or scissors. You can type in your choice. Please enter your choice (rock, paper, or scissors):

Example Input: > rock

System's Turn:

After you make your choice, the system will randomly select its own choice of rock, paper, or scissors.

Determine Winner:

The winner is determined by the following rules:
Rock crushes scissors. Scissors cut paper. Paper covers rock. If you and the system choose the same item, the game is a tie.

Display Results:

The system will then display the choices both you and it made, and announce the winner or declare a tie. Example Output:

You win !! Lucky guy. this is your 4 times win..

Play Again:

You will have the option to play again. Simply type yes to restart the game or no to exit.

Rules:

Choose your weapon wisely and remember the winning rules. Rock, Paper, and Scissors are equal; no one weapon is stronger than the others except in a head-to-head battle. Have fun and try to predict your opponent's choice!
